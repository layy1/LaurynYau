Frontline Real Estate Marketing Strategy

Developing a marketing strategy to increase engagement and applicant numbers.

Project Context

This project was submitted to Simon Fraser University's Student Marketing Association's (SMA) 2023 Brandstorm Case Competition partnering with Frontline Realestate Company to plan and design a marketing strategy to attract more potential hires and increase overall engagement.

Deliverables

A finalized brand strategy and related content mockups.

My Contribution

  • Conducted secondary research for the group to better understand Frontline Real Estate's target audience, current barriers and competitor strategies.
  • Fully filmed and edited video content mockups.
  • Prototyped web mockups.

Duration

4 Days (October-November 2023)

Tools

Figma, Adobe Premiere Pro

Team

Emma Ramsay, Megan Yung

Recognitions

Our team achieved second place in the case competition.

"It was really cool to see the care your team put into your project! Congratulations!" — Competition Judge

Marketing Stratgey

Combining video campaigns, social media posts and public graphics advertising networking events that streamlined audience members to a central interface. This central interface consisted of a geo-guesser game that highlighted locations Frontline Real Estate primarily works in, with a cameo of their office building to build 'hype' for prospective hires.

This strategy aims to increase Frontline’s social media follower-to-engagement ratio by 5% and attract new brokers.
