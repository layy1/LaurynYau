Captured: Short Film

Creating a thriller short film about a curious girl and a cult.

Project Context

This was an academic project, for a second year course New Media Images (IAT 202) at Simon Fraser University. The goal was to write and produce a short film using the proper equipment and software.

Deliverables

A 1-3 minute long short film.

My Contribution

I took initiative in organizing filming times and locations, recruiting cast members and developing the script and storyboard.

Duration

5 Weeks (October-November 2023)

Tools

Figma, Adobe Premiere Pro

Team

Areeba Ali, Emily Xu

Film Description

When a curious and reckless photographer goes to explore a secluded forest, she accidentally discovers a mysterious cult preparing for an unsettling ritual. Determined to capture their existence, she equips her camera and relentlessly chases after them.

Recognitions

'Captured' was chosen as one of the Top 10 final films, shown at our film showcase at the end of the term, and was awarded Best Sound.
